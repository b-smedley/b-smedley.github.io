Echoboard Support

Welcome to the support page for the Echoboard app. If you’re experiencing issues, please check the resources below or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a new sound?
    Open the app, tap the + button, and record or upload a sound file.
  • Why can’t I access premium themes?
    Premium themes are unlocked through an in-app purchase. Once purchased, restart the app to apply the new theme.
  • My sounds aren’t saving.
    Make sure you’ve granted storage permissions in your device settings.
