Help and FAQs for Echoboard

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline How do I add a new sound?
Open the app, tap the pencil button, and record or upload a sound file.
help_outline Why can’t I access premium themes?
Premium themes are unlocked through an in-app purchase. Once purchased, restart the app to apply the new theme.
help_outline My sounds aren’t saving.
Make sure you’ve granted storage permissions in your device settings.
help_outline How do I get more buttons?
Create a new board and choose your number of buttons, or go to the board chooser, click the 3 dots and edit the board.
help_outline How do I import or export my boards?

The app includes a built-in import/export feature to make it easy to back up or share your boards:

  • Export: The app zips all your boards and sounds into a single .zip package. You can then choose where to save it — either locally on your device or to a cloud service like Google Drive.
  • Import: Later, you can restore your boards by selecting the exported .zip file in the app’s Import option. The app will automatically unpack everything and load your boards and sounds back in.

This makes it simple to move your setup between devices or keep a safe backup of your custom boards.
