Privacy Policy for Echoboard

Echoboard ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Echoboard is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or address.

The types of information handled by the App include:

  • Audio Recordings: Sounds you record or upload are stored locally on your device. These files are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Usage Data: The App may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics for Firebase or advertising providers) to collect limited, anonymized information about how the App is used (e.g., device type, interactions, crash reports).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the core functionality of recording and playing sounds.
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience.
  • If analytics or ads are enabled, to help us understand general usage trends and support the free version of the App.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

  • Your audio files remain on your device unless you choose to share them manually.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.
  • Third-party services (like ads or analytics) may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (for displaying ads)
  • Firebase Analytics (for usage analytics)

5. Your Choices

  • You can remove ads by purchasing the premium version of the App.
  • You may delete your recordings at any time directly from the App.

6. Children’s Privacy

Echoboard is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the App and on the store listing.
